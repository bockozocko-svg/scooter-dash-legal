3.1 Anmeldung mit Google-Konto (optional)
Wenn du dich freiwillig mit deinem Google-Konto anmeldest, verarbeiten wir über den Dienst Firebase Authentication folgende Daten: angezeigter Name, eindeutige Nutzer-ID (UID) und ggf. 3.2 Spielstand (Spielwährung, freigeschaltete Fahrzeuge)
Bei Anmeldung mit Google speichern wir deinen Stand an Spielwährung sowie freigeschaltete und ausgewählte Fahrzeug-Skins in der Datenbank Cloud Firestore, verknüpft mit deiner Nutzer-ID. Diese Daten sind ausschließlich für dich selbst einsehbar.
Rechtsgrundlage: Vertragserfüllung (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. b DSGVO).

3.3 Globale Bestenliste (Highscores)
Beendest du eine Spielrunde, werden dein Name (Google-Anzeigename bzw. „Gast“), deine erzielte Punktzahl und der Zeitpunkt in einer öffentlichen, weltweit einsehbaren Bestenliste gespeichert.
Rechtsgrundlage: Einwilligung (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a DSGVO) durch Bestätigung der Nutzungsbedingungen.

3.4 Keine weiteren Daten
Die App verwendet keine Werbe-Netzwerke, kein Analytics- oder Tracking-Tool und keine Crash-Reporting-Dienste. Es werden keine Standortdaten, Kontakte oder Werbe-Identifikatoren erhoben.

5. Speicherdauer

  • Bestenlisten-Einträge werden dauerhaft gespeichert, bis eine Löschung beantragt wird.
  • Dein Spielstand bleibt gespeichert, solange dein Google-Konto mit der App verknüpft ist.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for data processing in connection with the "Scooter X Dash" app is:
bockozocko
Email: bockozocko@gmail.com

2. Overview

This privacy policy explains what personal data is processed when using the "Scooter X Dash" app, for what purpose, and what rights you have. The app can be played as a guest without signing in; in that case, no personal data is stored permanently.

3. What data is processed?

3.1 Google Sign-In (optional)
If you voluntarily sign in with your Google account, we process the following data via Firebase Authentication: display name, unique user ID (UID), and optionally a profile picture URL. This data is used to recognize you and sync your progress across devices.
Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by actively signing in with Google.

3.2 Game progress (in-game currency, unlocked scooters)
When signed in with Google, we store your in-game currency balance and unlocked/selected scooter skins in the Cloud Firestore database, linked to your user ID. This data is only visible to you.
Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

3.3 Global leaderboard (highscores)
When you finish a run, your name (Google display name or "Guest"), your score, and the timestamp are stored in a public, worldwide-visible leaderboard.
Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by accepting the terms of use.

3.4 No further data
The app uses no advertising networks, no analytics or tracking tools, and no crash-reporting services. No location data, contacts, or advertising identifiers are collected.

4. Recipients of data

For technical delivery we use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland (or Google LLC, USA), acting as our processor. More info: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Retention period

  • Leaderboard entries are stored permanently until deletion is requested.
  • Your progress remains stored as long as your Google account is linked to the app.
  • Guest progress is only kept locally for the duration of the session and not stored permanently.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. Contact bockozocko@gmail.com. As the app currently has no self-service deletion feature, deletion requests are handled manually and promptly upon contact.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, e.g. the Austrian Datenschutzbehörde (www.dsb.gv.at) or the authority of your country of residence.

7. Minors

The app is not specifically directed at children under 16. Users under 16 should only use the app with the consent of a parent or guardian, and only in guest mode (without Google sign-in).

8. Changes

We reserve the right to amend this privacy policy as needed. The version current at the time of your visit applies.
